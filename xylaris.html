About Xylaris

Xylaris is an alien ocean world deep within our galaxy, thriving without sunlight. It features:

  • Thick ice covering a vast ocean.
  • Life sustained by chemosynthesis from hydrothermal vents.
  • Extreme conditions similar to icy moons like Europa.
